Put your home-page card images in this folder.

Supported naming:
  Categories card:        categories-1.jpg (or .png/.webp), categories-2.jpg, ...
  Recent Additions card:  recent-additions-1.jpg, recent-additions-2.jpg, ...
  Browse By Series card:  browse-by-series-1.jpg, ...
  Browse By Genre card:   browse-by-genre-1.jpg, ...
  All card:               all-1.jpg, ...

If you only want ONE image per card, you can also use:
  categories.jpg, recent-additions.jpg, browse-by-series.jpg, browse-by-genre.jpg, all.jpg

Tip: you need at least TWO images for a card before the Shuffle button can visibly change it.
